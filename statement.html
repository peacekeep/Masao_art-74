Artist Statement

Hello my name is Masao Kajiyama, student at San Jose state . I am majoring in art because I like to draw,paint, and making videos of my own. As an artist,I care about the effort and quality put into my works. I also care about the audience when working on a project. When it comes to making video, I like to have it be based on my own hobbies or montage.Most of my video work I do involves series of dancing footage or building process of a model kit.
