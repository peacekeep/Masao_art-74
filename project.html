Passion, 2018

This is a time lapse video of me building a plastic model kit. It is an long tedious process to snap the pieces together as well as gluing and smoothing out individual parts. I decided to Switch the footage audio with a fitting music. I titled this video passion because It's something that I like to do on my spare time other than drawing

tribute to Levan Polka, 2018

I used videos of dancing anime fictional character from youtube and my own footage of a video game I recorded using my DSLR camera. I used premiere for editing and combining my own footage with found footage. The song I used is called Levan Polka in tribute to Miku which fits with the the footage. The purpose of this video is to have the viewer get the feeling of being in a stress free space.
